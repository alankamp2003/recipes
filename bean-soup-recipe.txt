1.  Slice onions.
2.  Slice other vegetables e.g. egg plant, tomatoes, bell-peppers etc.
3.  Heat oil in a skillet.
4.  Fry the onions.
5.  Add tomato puree and the peppers.
6.  Mix chilli powder and some other spices and condiments if you want.
6.  Fry the stuff for a couple of minutes.
7.  Pour some water into a pitcher.
8.  Pour beans and the fried vegetables in the water.
9.  Heat for about 5 minutes on high, churning the contents frequently.
10. Mix salt.
11. Drink the soup if you dare.
